SPRING BREAK to Aruba: A happy island

It's easy here. To wander freely and safely. To embrace the warm sun and warmer people. To lose yourself in what you love and find happiness. Solo ta brilla. The sun is shining. It’s something we Arubans say more than anyone else in the Caribbean.
